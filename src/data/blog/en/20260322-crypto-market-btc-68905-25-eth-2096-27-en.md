## 2. Technical Factors Behind Major‑Coin Performance  

### 2.1 Bitcoin: Technical Resistance Around $70,000  
- **Resistance levels**: $70,000; $71,500 (previous high)  
- **Support levels**: $68,200 (today’s low); $66,500 (long‑term support)  
- **On‑chain signal**: Active BTC address count fell 3.2 % this week, but whales still hold >45 % of supply, showing institutions are watching closely.  

### 2.2 Ethereum: Upgrade Expectations Drive Volatility  
- **EIP‑4844 (Proto‑Danksharding)** is slated for **Q3 2024**. Anticipation of fee compression lifts ETH’s fundamental appeal.  
- **Short‑term pressure**: The recent **2.75 %** dip is mainly a brief reaction to “Ethereum 2.0” news.  

### 2.3 Other Major Chains: Relative Performance of BNB, SOL, AVAX  
- **BNB**: Benefiting from Binance’s fee‑discount program, it holds near the $630 zone with support at $620.  
- **SOL**: Solana’s ecosystem recently launched **“Neon EVM,”** but network congestion raised fees, nudging the price down to $87.71.  
- **AVAX**: A slowdown in the **“Subnet”** project has weakened sentiment, pushing the token down close to 4 %.

- **Near**: The upcoming **“Shardnet”** in Q2 aims to improve scaling, likely providing price support.

- **Technical risk**: Delays or security bugs in on‑chain upgrades (e.g., ETH Shanghai, Solana Neon) would likely exert negative price pressure.
